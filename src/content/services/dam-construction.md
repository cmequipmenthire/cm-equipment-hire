Farm dams are critical infrastructure for Wheatbelt properties — stock water, crop spraying, fire suppression, and irrigation all depend on a dam that holds water reliably year after year. Whether you need a new dam built from the ground up, an existing dam cleaned out and restored, or an older dam enlarged to meet growing demands, we have the equipment and experience to deliver.

## New Dam Construction

We build both excavated dams and turkey nest dams, matching the design to your soil type, catchment area, and intended use. Our Cat 336E excavator handles the dig work while the Cat 740 dump truck moves spoil efficiently — meaning your dam is built to spec without unnecessary delays. We pay attention to compaction, bank grades, and spillway design because a dam that leaks or overtops isn't a dam worth building.

## Dam Clean-Outs and Enlargements

Over time, every dam silts up. The catchment washes sediment in, the floor rises, and your storage capacity drops. A proper clean-out restores your dam to its original depth and capacity — and it's a fraction of the cost of building new. If your needs have outgrown your existing dam, we can deepen and enlarge it at the same time.

## Spillways and Bank Work

Spillways protect your dam from overtopping and bank failure during heavy rainfall events. We construct and repair spillways as part of new builds and as standalone jobs. Bank reshaping and compaction work is also available for dams showing signs of erosion or settlement.

Note: Depending on the size and location of your dam, you may need a licence from the Department of Water and Environmental Regulation (DWER). We're happy to point you in the right direction if you're unsure about the requirements.
