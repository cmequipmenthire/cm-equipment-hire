Good drainage is the difference between productive paddocks and waterlogged headaches. Across the Wheatbelt, uncontrolled surface water strips topsoil, drowns crops, causes salinity, and turns access tracks into bogs. A well-designed contour bank and drainage system manages that water — keeping it where it helps and directing it safely where it doesn't.

## Contour Bank Installation

We install both grade banks and level banks to suit your property's specific needs. The Cat 336E excavator builds banks with clean, consistent profiles that are easy to maintain and don't interfere with modern broadacre farming equipment. Whether you're protecting a newly cleared paddock or upgrading drainage on established cropping country, we build to surveyed designs and get the levels right.

## Contour Bank Removal and Reshaping

Farming has changed. Many older bank systems were designed for different tillage practices and smaller machinery. If your existing contours are causing waterlogging, blocking efficient paddock layouts, or simply not doing their job, we can remove them cleanly and reshape the ground for re-cropping. Our gear leaves a smooth finish — no leftover ridges or low spots to catch the seeder bar.

## Drainage Channels and Waterways

Sometimes banks alone aren't enough. We construct drainage channels and grassed waterways to safely move larger volumes of water off paddocks and into dams, natural watercourses, or disposal areas. Combined with contour banks, a proper waterway system protects your soil and your investment in the crop.

## Surface Water Management

Every property is different. We work with farmers to plan earthworks that address their specific water management challenges — whether that's a single problem area or a whole-property drainage overhaul. If you're not sure what you need, we're happy to walk the paddock with you and talk through the options.
